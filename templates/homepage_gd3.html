Look through the menuand find some food you are interested in.

Are you curious about the calories of each food you are going to eat or have eaten today? Try the "Calorie" function by switching the dropdown into "Calorie". Type a food name and click on the "Search" button to see a predicted calorie count of it.

Do you have any food restrictions? Try the "Allergy" function by switching the dropdown into "Allergy". Type a food name or several food names seperated by comma and click on the "Search" button to see all the allergens contained by it/them.
